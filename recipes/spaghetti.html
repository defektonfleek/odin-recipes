Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients
  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Red wine
  • Italian herbs
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps
  1. Sautéing Aromatics:
    • Sauté onions, garlic, carrots, and celery until softened.
    • Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  2. Making the Sauce:
    • Pour in tomato sauce and red wine.
    • Season with Italian herbs and simmer until flavors meld.
  3. Cooking Spaghetti:
    • Boil spaghetti until al dente.
    • Drain and toss with Bolognese sauce.
  4. Serve and Enjoy:
    • Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and serve hot.
    • Pair with a glass of red wine for the perfect Italian meal.

Spaghetti Bolognese, a classic Italian comfort dish, is sure to satisfy your cravings for hearty and flavorful pasta.
