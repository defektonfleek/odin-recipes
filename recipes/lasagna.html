Lasagna

Ingredients
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground chicken
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Garlic, onions and herbs for flavor

Steps
  1. Cooking the Meat Sauce
    • Brown the ground meat with onions and garlic.
    • Add tomato sauce and simmer with herbs for a robust flavor.
  2. Creating Béchamel Sauce:
    • Melt butter, stir in flour, and gradually add milk.
    • Cook until the sauce thickens, enhancing the lasagna's creaminess.
  3. Layering the Lasagna:
    • Boil lasagna noodles until al dente.
    • In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, béchamel, and a trio of cheeses.
  4. Baking to Perfection:
    • Repeat the layers and top with a final layer of cheese.
    • Bake until golden and bubbly, allowing the flavors to meld.
  5. Serve and Enjoy:
    • Let it rest before slicing to savor the layers of flavor.
    • Pair with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete meal.

Classic Lasagna is not just a dish; it's a celebration of comforting textures and harmonious flavors. Share this culinary masterpiece with friends and family, creating unforgettable moments around the dining table. Buon Appetito!
