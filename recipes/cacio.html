Cacio e Pepe

Ingredients
  • Spaghetti
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps
  1. Cooking the Pasta:
    • Boil spaghetti until al dente.
    • Reserve a cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.
  2. Creating the Sauce:
    • Grate Pecorino Romano cheese and set aside.
    • In a pan, combine olive oil and black pepper over low heat.
    • Add cooked spaghetti to the pan and toss.
    • Gradually add grated cheese, stirring to create a creamy sauce. If needed, use reserved pasta water to adjust consistency.
  3. Serving:
    • Plate the Cacio e Pepe and sprinkle with additional cheese and black pepper.
    • Drizzle with a bit of olive oil and serve immediately.

Cacio e Pepe, a Roman classic, brings together simple yet bold flavors with its rich Pecorino Romano cheese and a generous dose of black pepper. Enjoy the elegance of this pasta dish!
